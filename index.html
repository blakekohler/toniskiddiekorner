‚ú® Our Story

Founded on a Dream to Inspire Young Learners

The Preschool Place began in 2013 when Toni pursued her dream of creating a play-based preschool in her basement. What started with just a few children has grown into multiple locations across Eagle Mountain and Saratoga Springs.

In 2015, Megan joined the team, and together they've built a curriculum that perfectly balances kindergarten readiness with the developmental needs of young children. Today, our team of passionate educators continues to expand this vision to new communities.
